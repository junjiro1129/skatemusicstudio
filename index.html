FAQ

Is the music AI-generated?
Yes — and that is a feature, not a limitation. AI composition means your skater receives a score that has never existed before, with no copyright strings attached. At the 2026 Milan Olympics, AI-generated music was used in official ISU competition for the first time. The landscape is evolving rapidly, and SMS is built for it.
Is it safe to use at ISU competitions?
Yes. Every SMS score is 100% original — not based on any existing copyrighted work. There is currently no ISU rule prohibiting AI-generated music. Each delivery includes a Certificate of Rights confirming the track is royalty-free and cleared for ISU events, YouTube, and TV broadcasting worldwide.
What is the difference between Standard and Custom Synchronized Score?
The Standard Original Score ($199/track) is composed to your genre, BPM, and duration — ideal when choreography has not yet been set. The Custom Synchronized Score ($450/track) is composed and refined to match an existing choreography precisely, aligning key musical moments with specific moves. Unlimited revisions are included until you are fully satisfied.
What file formats are delivered?
Upon payment, you receive fully mastered .wav (CD quality) and .mp3 (320kbps) files — both suitable for competition submission and practice. Tracks shared during the selection process are unmastered previews.
What languages can I communicate in?
English and Japanese. All inquiries are handled personally by the founder. We respond within 24 hours.
